About Goonj Certificate Generator

What is Goonj Certificate Generator?

Goonj Certificate Generator is an intelligent web application designed to revolutionize the process of creating digital certificates. With AI-powered field detection and smart template scanning, creating perfectly aligned certificates has never been easier. Whether you need a single certificate or bulk generation for hundreds of participants, our platform makes it effortless.

Smart Certificate Generator

Our flagship feature uses advanced AI and OCR technology to automatically detect text fields in your certificate templates. Simply upload your template, let the AI analyze it, enter your custom values, and download your perfectly aligned certificate!

How It Works

  1. Upload Your Template: Simply upload your certificate template (PNG, JPG, or PDF)
  2. AI Scans the Template: Our AI analyzes the template and detects text fields automatically
  3. Enter Field Values: Review detected fields and enter the values you want
  4. Generate & Download: Get your perfectly aligned certificate instantly!

Bulk Generation via Telegram Bot

For bulk certificate generation, we offer a convenient Telegram bot interface. The bot allows you to:

  • Create events with certificate templates
  • Upload participant lists via CSV files
  • Generate and email certificates to all participants automatically
  • Track job progress in real-time

To use the Telegram bot, simply search for our bot on Telegram and use the following commands:

  • /start - Get started with the bot
  • /newjob - Create a new certificate generation job
  • /status <job_id> - Check job status
  • /events - List available events

Technology Stack

Built with modern, reliable technologies:

  • Backend: Flask (Python)
  • Database: MongoDB with TTL indexing for efficient data management
  • AI/OCR: Tesseract OCR, OpenCV, pytesseract for intelligent field detection
  • Certificate Generation: Pillow (PIL) for high-quality image processing
  • Email: Flask-Mail for automated delivery
  • Bot: python-telegram-bot for Telegram integration
