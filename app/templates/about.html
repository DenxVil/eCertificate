About Goonj Certificate Generator

What is Goonj Certificate Generator?

Goonj Certificate Generator is an intelligent web application designed to revolutionize the process of creating and distributing digital certificates. With AI-powered field detection and smart template scanning, creating perfectly aligned certificates has never been easier. Whether you're organizing a workshop, webinar, course, or any event that requires participant certificates, our platform makes it effortless.

How Smart Generator Works

  1. Upload Your Template: Simply upload your certificate template (PNG, JPG, or PDF)
  2. AI Scans the Template: Our AI analyzes the template and detects text fields automatically
  3. Enter Field Values: Review detected fields and enter the values you want
  4. Generate & Download: Get your perfectly aligned certificate instantly!

Traditional Workflow

  1. Create an event and upload a certificate template
  2. Create a job for the event and upload participant data (CSV/Excel)
  3. The system automatically generates certificates for each participant
  4. Certificates are emailed to participants automatically
  5. Track job progress and view generation statistics

Telegram Bot

Our Telegram bot provides a convenient interface for certificate generation:

  • /start - Get started with the bot
  • /newjob - Create a new certificate generation job
  • /status <job_id> - Check job status
  • /events - List available events
